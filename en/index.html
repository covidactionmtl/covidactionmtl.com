About us

COVID Action Montréal is a mutual aid and disability justice collective that offers free high quality masks to help prevent the spread of COVID and other airborne illnesses. We work to raise awareness of the effects of COVID, how to best protect yourself and others, and advocate for vulnerable members of our communities to not just survive, but to thrive.

Free Masks

Need a mask? No problem, Covid Action Montréal provides free masks and rapid tests (tests when available) for all in Tiohtià:ke / Mooniyaang / Montréal. Check the map below for current pickup locations. If there is nothing near you or you would like to become a pickup location feel free to contact us. Got a community event that needs masks? We’ve got you covered for that also. Contact us to organize getting masks for your event and also check out our resources on organizing COVID safer events.

Donate A mask

If you’re looking for a large amount of masks, we encourage you to make a request with Donate A Mask. This is a great organisation that sends free masks to people and groups across Canada. Delivery can take a few weeks, so we recommend placing your order two months before the masks are needed.

Air Purifier Lending Library

We recommend hosting events outdoors to reduce the spread of airborne illness but sometimes events have to be held indoors and masked. For small venues, we have several small air purifiers that can be borrowed for free to help make your event more accessible for vulnerable members of our community. Fill out the form below to request an air filter for your event.
